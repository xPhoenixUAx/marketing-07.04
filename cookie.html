This Cookie Policy describes how PRIMESET LIMITED uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, understand user behavior, support analytics, and improve user experience.

How We Use Cookies

Essential functionality

Some cookies may support basic website functionality, navigation consistency, and technical stability.

Analytics

We may use analytics cookies to understand page visits, traffic sources, session behavior, and general engagement trends so we can improve content and usability.

Performance and optimization

We may use cookies to evaluate which pages, calls to action, and pathways are most useful so we can refine the website experience over time.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed or read by third-party services such as analytics, scheduling, CRM, or marketing technology providers. These third parties may process data according to their own policies and terms.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that restricting certain cookies may affect website functionality, preferences, or measurement accuracy.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in website functionality, technology usage, or legal requirements. The current version will always be posted on this page.
