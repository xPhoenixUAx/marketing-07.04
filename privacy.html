01. Who We Are

This website is operated by PRIMESET LIMITED (Company No. HE 438954), a company registered in the Republic of Cyprus, with its registered office at:

Pavlou Nirvana 4, Alpha Tower, 1st Floor, Office 13,
3021 Limassol, Cyprus

We provide digital marketing, advertising, and related business support services to companies across Europe and internationally.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and applicable Cyprus data protection legislation, PRIMESET LIMITED acts as the data controller in respect of personal data collected through this website.

We are committed to protecting the personal data of our website visitors, prospective clients, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

02. Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you interact with our website, submit a form, communicate with us, or engage our services.

The types of data we may collect include:

Identity data — name, surname, company name, job title.

Contact data — email address, phone number, business address.

Financial data — invoicing details, VAT numbers, and billing-related information (where applicable).

Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, time zone, and system data.

Usage data — pages visited, time on site, navigation paths, interactions.

Marketing data — preferences for receiving communications and campaign-related interactions.

Business data — information shared in relation to services (e.g. marketing goals, budgets, project scope).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data. If such data is provided, it will be deleted where appropriate.

03. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and contact requests
  • To provide and improve our services
  • To manage client relationships and communications
  • To process invoices and business transactions
  • To analyse website performance and user behaviour
  • To support marketing and advertising activities
  • To ensure website security and prevent misuse
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not use your data for unrelated purposes without informing you.

04. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases:

Contract performance — when processing is necessary to provide services

Legitimate interests — to improve services, ensure security, and operate our business

Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws and regulations

Consent — where required (e.g. marketing communications, cookies)

You may withdraw consent at any time.

05. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Client and financial data — up to 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Inquiry data — up to 2 years
  • Analytics data — up to 26 months
  • Marketing data — until consent is withdrawn

We may anonymise data for analytical purposes.

06. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, CRM, email tools)
  • Advertising platforms (Google, Meta)
  • Professional advisers (legal, accounting)
  • Authorities where required by law

All third parties are required to handle data securely and lawfully.

07. International Transfers

Some providers (e.g. Google, Meta) may process data outside the EEA.

Where this occurs, we use appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Approved data transfer mechanisms

08. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Receive your data (portability)
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Cyprus Data Protection Authority.

09. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyse usage
  • Support marketing efforts

For full details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
