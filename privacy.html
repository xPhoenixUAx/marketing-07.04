Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Northstar Metric collects, uses, stores, and protects information provided through our website and business communications.

Privacy PolicyTerms of ServiceCookie Policy

Information Collected

Contact information

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, website, and any details you submit through our contact forms or direct communications.

Usage data

We may collect standard website usage information such as browser type, device information, referring pages, page views, and session behavior through analytics tools.

Business context

If you contact us for services, we may receive information about your marketing goals, budget considerations, internal operations, and current performance challenges.

How Information Is Used

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, evaluate project fit, deliver services, improve website performance, maintain security, and communicate relevant business updates.

We may also use information to understand what services visitors are most interested in so we can improve the clarity and usefulness of our website content.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to support analytics, remember preferences, and understand how visitors interact with pages and forms. You can review more detail in our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Tools

We may use trusted third-party platforms for analytics, communications, scheduling, CRM, and performance reporting. These providers may process data on our behalf subject to their own privacy and security practices.

Data Protection

We take commercially reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or loss. No internet transmission or storage environment can be guaranteed as fully secure, but we maintain practical safeguards appropriate for a professional services business.

User Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information where applicable by contacting us at hello@northstarmetric.com. We will respond in accordance with applicable law and reasonable business requirements.

Retention and Updates

We retain information only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes, contractual obligations, compliance requirements, and recordkeeping needs. We may update this policy from time to time, and the updated version will be posted on this page.
